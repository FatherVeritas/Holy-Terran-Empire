About This World

It is the year 3500 AD. The Catholic Church has once more risen to her rightful primacy, and the Emperor of the Holy Terran Empire, united with the Pontifex of Rome, strives to bring the Light of Christ to the farthest reaches of the galaxy. Ships cross the void at faster-than-light speeds through Etherspace. Alien races — descendants of mankind, seeded among the stars at the Astersion — await the Gospel, longing for redemption. Standing ever vigilant are the religious-military orders of the Battlemonks: celibate warriors sworn to defend the galaxy. Yet even as the faith spreads, that ancient enemy of mankind, The Devil and Satan, ceaselessly sows discord, heresy, and rebellion among the peoples of the stars.
